About Us

"We want the unheard to be heard and make a safe place for people to report and make the campus safe and prevent any problems."

Our Story

We are a group of students from Sto. Niño National High School who saw the need for a safe, confidential way for students and staff to speak up about issues affecting our campus community. Too often, voices go unheard and problems go unaddressed because people are afraid to speak up.

Our Mission

The SNNHS Campus Guard was created to give everyone a voice. Whether it's reporting concerns about safety, substance use, or any issue that threatens our wellbeing, we believe that speaking up is an act of courage and care. Every report helps us build a stronger, safer, and more supportive school environment.

Our Promise

  • Your identity is protected - you can report anonymously
  • Reports are reviewed within 24 hours
  • We focus on help and intervention, not punishment
